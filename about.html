Our Story

Pioneering sustainable mining solutions since 2010

Who We Are

MG Australia was founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize the mining industry through sustainable practices and cutting-edge technology. What began as a small team of passionate geologists and engineers has grown into a nationally recognized leader in mineral exploration and extraction. Our journey has been guided by an unwavering commitment to environmental stewardship and community engagement.

Over the past decade, we've successfully completed over 200 projects across Australia, each demonstrating our ability to balance economic viability with ecological responsibility. Our team now comprises 150+ specialists across geology, engineering, environmental science, and community relations. This diverse expertise allows us to approach mining challenges from multiple perspectives, ensuring comprehensive solutions.

Unlike traditional mining companies, we've invested heavily in renewable energy integration, developing proprietary systems that reduce our operations' carbon footprint by up to 60%. Our innovative reclamation techniques have restored over 5,000 hectares of land to thriving ecosystems, earning us multiple sustainability awards. We measure our success not just in mineral yields, but in positive environmental and social impact.

At the heart of our operations is a culture of continuous improvement. We allocate 15% of annual profits to research and development, partnering with universities and tech startups to pioneer cleaner extraction methods. Our recent breakthroughs in bioleaching and drone-based surveying have set new industry standards for efficiency and minimal environmental disruption.

Looking ahead, we're committed to achieving net-zero emissions across all operations by 2035. This ambitious goal drives our current investments in hydrogen-powered equipment and AI-driven resource optimization. For MG Australia, mining isn't just about what we extract - it's about the legacy we leave through responsible innovation and meaningful community partnerships.

Our Leadership Team

Rosario Choquenaira Florez

CEO & Founder

Rosario is the CEO and Founder of MG Australia, a company committed to strengthening and advancing mining operations and partnerships between Peru and Australia.

With a deep understanding of the mining industry and a strong vision for innovation and sustainability, Rosario established MG Australia to bridge the gap between two of the world's most resource-rich countries. Her leadership is focused on improving the efficiency, quality, and global collaboration of mining processes — from resource development to export logistics.

Rosario brings a unique perspective shaped by her Peruvian heritage and her experience in the Australian sector. She is dedicated to building stronger international connections, promoting ethical practices, and driving progress across both continents.

Under her guidance, MG Australia is not only a company — it is a mission to create smarter, more sustainable mining solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Leader of MG Australia

With a strong background in leadership, international relations, and cross-border collaboration, Luis brings strategic vision and direction to MG Australia. His work is focused on advancing partnerships that benefit both nations, particularly in the mining, trade, and innovation sectors.

His diplomatic expertise allows MG Australia to form strong relationships with key partners and expand its impact both locally and internationally.

Luis is passionate about creating opportunities, building sustainable industries, and inspiring future generations through meaningful international cooperation.
