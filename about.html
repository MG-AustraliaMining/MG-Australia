Our Story

Pioneering sustainable mining solutions since 2010

Who We Are

MG Australia was founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize the mining industry through sustainable practices and cutting-edge technology. What began as a small team of passionate geologists and engineers has grown into a nationally recognized leader in mineral exploration and extraction.

Our journey has been guided by an unwavering commitment to environmental stewardship and community engagement. We measure our success not just in mineral yields, but in positive environmental and social impact, creating smarter, more sustainable mining solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Our Leadership Team

Rosario Choquenaira Florez

CEO & Founder

Rosario is the CEO and Founder of MG Australia, dedicated to enhancing and modernising mining collaboration between Peru and Australia. With a strong vision for sustainable growth and international partnership, she leads the company with a focus on innovation, efficiency, and ethical industry practices.

Her leadership bridges two resource-rich nations, aiming to create long-term benefits for both communities and the environment.

Luis Enrique Yhui Jordan

Leader of MG Australia

Luis is the Leader of MG Australia and CEO of the Peru Australia WA Consular, where he focuses on strengthening diplomatic, economic, and cultural ties between Peru and Western Australia.

With extensive experience in international relations and strategic leadership, he guides MG Australia's mission to connect industries across continents, particularly in the mining sector, while promoting cooperation, sustainability, and long-term development.

Gerard Enrique Yhui Choquenaira

Software Designer

Gerard is the Software Designer at MG Australia, with advanced coding skills in Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and C++. At just 10 years old, he brings exceptional creativity and technical ability to the team.

Passionate about technology and committed to learning, Gerard plays a key role in shaping MG Australia's software systems, proving that innovation knows no age.
